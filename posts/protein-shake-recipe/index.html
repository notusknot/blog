Peanut butter protein shake recipe

This is a simple peanut butter protein shake recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8oz almond milk (replacement: regular milk, oat milk, some other milk replacement)
  • Frozen banana (replacement: unfrozen banana)
  • 2tbsp peanut butter (replacement: 2tbsp peanut powder, almond butter)
  • 2tbsp chia seeds (replacement: flaxseed meal?)
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • Small amount of cinnamon

I think this shake tastes really good and is a pretty nutritious way to start the day. Usually the whole process including prep and cleaning only takes around 10 minutes for me.
